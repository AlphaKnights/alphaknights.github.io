ABOUT US

who are we?

We are the Alpha Knights, a first-time FIRST Robotics team based in San Marcos High School, California (not the one in Texas). Our team is composed of the very best programmers, artists, leaders, and hardware experts at our school. Together, our goal is not only to win — but to gain valuable experience and bond together over time.
