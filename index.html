ABOUT US

who are we?

We are the Alpha Knights, a first-time FIRST Robotics team based in San Marcos High School, California — Team 6695. Our team is composed of the very best programmers, artists, leaders, and hardware experts at our school. Together, our goal is not only to win — but to gain valuable experience and bond together over time.

President, Captain

James Conde is a senior,18, and about to start the next phase of this life in college. He is also the founding Captain of San Marcos’s Inaugural First Robotics Team. He is very proud to be starting a program that he hopes will provide valuable experience and scholarships to the intrepid students who take on the First Robotics challenge.
